Research interests

Publications

Multistage optimization of a petroleum production system with material balance model (2022)

With Jean-Philippe Chancelier, Michel De Lara, Pierre Carpentier, Alejandro Rodriguez Martinez and Anna Robert.
In this article, we present a new formulation of the reservoir in a petroleum production system as a controlled (non-linear) dynamical system by using material balance equations, under the standard assumptions that the fluids follow a black-oil model and that the reservoir has a tank-like behavior.
